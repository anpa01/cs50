Who is Anakin Skywalker

Anakin Skywalker is the main character of Star Wars episodes 1 to 6. Episodes 1 through 6 show Anakin's growth as he goes from a boy on Tatooine, to a Jedi Knight(a good guy), to a Sith Lord(a bad guy), to a person who cares about his family.
